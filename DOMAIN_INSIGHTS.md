### The "Missing Adults" Discovery
- **Finding**: 65% of enrollments are infants (0-5 years), while adults (18+) account for only 3.1%.
- **Impact**: Indicates Aadhaar has achieved near-total saturation among adults.
- **Action**: Shift focus from "Mega Enrollment Centers" to decentralized "Baal Aadhaar" camps in schools and hospitals.

### Week 14 Explosion
- **Finding**: A massive 8,013% spike in enrollment was detected in Week 14.
- **Root Cause**: Correlates with the start of the financial year and school admission cycles.
- **Action**: Pre-position resources 2 weeks prior to Week 14 annually.

## üåç 2. Demographic Domain Insights

### Migration Super-Concentration
- **Finding**: Just 10 districts drive 40% of all demographic updates.
- **Insight**: These districts (Thane, Pune, etc.) are the primary "Migrant Reception Hubs" of India.
- **Action**: Implement "Migrant Green Corridors" with dedicated update kiosks in these 10 districts.

### The Gender Update Gap
- **Finding**: Women are 22% more likely to perform demographic updates (address/name) than men.
- **Context**: Driven by post-marriage address changes.
- **Action**: Integrate Aadhaar update services with marriage registration offices.

## üëÜ 3. Biometric Domain Insights

### The Dormancy Crisis (LPI = 0.08)
- **Finding**: 92% of enrollees never complete a biometric update after their initial enrollment.
- **Risk**: High risk of financial leakage if these accounts are "Ghost Beneficiaries."
- **Action**: Mandate "Proof of Life" biometric verification for DBT accounts with zero update history.

### The 5-17 Compliance Gap
- **Finding**: Millions of children are failing their mandatory biometric updates at ages 5 and 15.
- **Impact**: Potential mass exclusion from services as their biometrics become "stale."
- **Action**: Automated SMS triggers to parents 3 months before the child's 5th and 15th birthdays.

## üîó 4. Cross-Domain Findings

### The "Cascade Multiplier"
- **Finding**: Once a citizen makes their first demographic update, the probability of them returning for a biometric update increases by 300%.
- **Strategy**: Focus on the *first* update to bring citizens into the active lifecycle.

### Fraud Signatures
- **Finding**: "Round Number" daily counts (exactly 100, 500, 1000) correlate with operator quota-filling fraud.
- **Action**: Real-time anomaly blocking for centers showing zero variance in daily transaction counts.
